📄 Complaint Form — Kaise Bharein?

Cybercrime portal pe complaint bharne ki step-by-step guide aur printable forms download karein

💳 Financial Fraud Complaint

UPI fraud, banking fraud, online scam ke liye

1
cybercrime.gov.in kholein → "Report Cyber Crime" click karein
2
Category: "Financial Fraud" chunein → Sub-category select karein
3
Naam, mobile, email bharein (ya anonymous option chunein)
4
Bank details, transaction ID, amount fill karein
5
Screenshots upload → Submit → Complaint ID note karein

👤 Harassment / Identity Theft

Social media hack, morphing, stalking ke cases ke liye

1
"Women & Child" ya "Cyber Crime Against Women" category chunein
2
Incident date, platform (Instagram/Facebook/WhatsApp) batayein
3
Accused ka profile link / phone number dein (agar pata ho)
4
Screenshots, URLs evidence upload karein
5
Nearest thana naam dein → Submit → Tracking ID save karein

🖥️ Hacking / Data Breach

Account hack, ransomware, phishing attacks ke liye

1
"Other Cyber Crime" → "Hacking / Damage to Systems" chunein
2
Kaunsa device ya account hack hua — uski details bharein
3
Date & time of incident clearly mention karein
4
Server logs, email headers attach karein agar ho
5
CERT-In ko bhi parallel report karein: cert-in.org.in

What You Can Report

Types of cyber crimes that can be reported

Financial Fraud

  • • UPI/Paytm fraud
  • • Credit/debit card fraud
  • • Banking fraud
  • • Investment scams
  • • Lottery/prize scams

Online Crimes

  • • Phishing attacks
  • • Identity theft
  • • Email hacking
  • • Social media fraud
  • • Data theft

Harassment

  • • Cyber stalking
  • • Online bullying
  • • Morphing images
  • • Defamation
  • • Threats and intimidation

Documents to Keep Ready

For Financial Frauds:

  • Bank statements showing fraudulent transactions
  • Screenshots of payment confirmations
  • SMS/email communications with fraudster
  • Copy of FIR (if filed with local police)

For Other Cyber Crimes:

  • Screenshots of offensive content
  • URLs of offending websites/posts
  • Details of the accused (if known)
  • Witness information (if any)
