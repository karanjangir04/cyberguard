About CYBERGUARD

CYBERGUARD is a comprehensive cybersecurity awareness initiative under the ISEA (Information Security Education and Awareness) project, supported by CDAC and the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of India.

Our mission is to create a cyber-resilient society by educating citizens about digital threats, providing real-time scam detection tools, and facilitating easy reporting of cyber crimes.
